DataBeers Exeter #1

Venue: Positive Lights Project, Exeter

Since joining the University of Exeter, Federico and Ronaldo had long discussed bringing DataBeers to the city. The idea gained momentum but the pandemic put everything on hold. The belief that data science is a skill for everyone never faded though, and eventually the right time, location, format, and speakers all came together for our inaugural event.
