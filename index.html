DataBeers Exeter

Where data meets community, over a pint.

An informal, PechaKucha-style meetup bringing together data scientists, analysts, engineers and curious minds from across Devon and beyond. Grab a beer, share your work, and connect with the local data community.

Find out more Past events

What to expect

Informal atmosphere

No suits, no slides required. Just people passionate about data gathering in a welcoming pub setting.

PechaKucha talks

Short, punchy PechaKucha-style talks on anything data-related. 20 slides, 20 seconds each. Projects, tools, experiments, failures.

Based in Exeter

Rooted in Devon's county city, drawing speakers and attendees from across the South West.

Want to Speak?

We are always looking for speakers. Beginners very welcome. If you have done something interesting with data, we want to hear about it.
